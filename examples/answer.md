The design doc says reads hit a cache first: "Reads are served from a
write-through cache in front of the primary store" [1].

Cache entries expire after 300 seconds and a background sweeper enforces
expiry once per minute [1].

According to the billing doc, "Invoices are generated on the first business
day of each month" [1].

The cache absorbed 97% of read traffic during the 2025 load test [1].

Enterprise revenue grew 40% year over year thanks to the new rebate
program [2].
